Hello,

My name is Amber Lin. I have recently graduated from University of Michigan, School of Information with Human Computer Interaction (HCI) and Library and Information Science (LIS) specialization. Currently, I am seeking a full-time position as an UX designer or researcher.

I am passionate about observing people. I love to observe their behavior, understand their thoughts, and further design solutions in response to their needs. I believe people are the central of good design products; understand our users is the first step for creating the best user experience. Also, I like to collaborate with diverse people, it always helps me learn more ideas and think outside the box.

Dancing, music and travel enrich my life. I play piano for more than ten years, and have danced many years since I was in the college. Traveling always inspires me some ideas; meeting with different cultures and people always get my energy back to life. Making some handicraft is my amateur interest, I started years ago and kept on creating new design works.

If you are interested in knowing more about me, welcome to grab my resume and contact me !
