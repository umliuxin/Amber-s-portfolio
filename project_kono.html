This is the project I worked at Kono. We were developing a social tool named Quotelet. Quotelet helps user easily and quickly collect the quote they like, edit and share it with friends when they are reading articles online. So far, it is a plug-in extension that installed in Google Chrome. However, based on our user research, we were thinking to develop a mobile or a tablet version in the future.


My Responsibilities


• Conducted diary studies and interviews to understand target users’ habit of online reading and their experience with Quotelet

• Created wireframes and overall interaction flow
