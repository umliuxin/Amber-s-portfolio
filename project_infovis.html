Oscar Best Picture

Information Visualization

Date:

Jan 2014 - Apr. 2014

Team Member:

Shih-Hsuan Chou, Xin liu, Chengqi Zhu, Changcheng Qian

My Role:

UX Designer

Tools:

D3 / SVG, JavaScript, Python

We used the data set of Oscar Best Picture movies, including winners and nominees from 1929 to 2014 and compared different variables, including rating, votes, budget, gross, and genre. We designed this visualization for users who want to find the trend and characteristics of Oscar Best Picture movies.

We did three visualizations to show the full context that corresponds to our use case:

  • An animated bubble chart shows all Oscar Best Picture movies to see the quantity and gross of each genre
  • An interactive scatter plot shows the relation among gross, budget and rating for all winners and nominees
  • A multi­series line chart shows the comparison of all movies or winners/nominees by the five variables
