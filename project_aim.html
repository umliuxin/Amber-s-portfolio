I am currently a UX/UI designer at Alliance Inspection Management (AiM) - JDP team, striving to create several mobile applications to make car inspectors’ life easier.


AiM is a premier new and pre-owned vehicle inspection company with 500+ inspectors nationwide. Currently, car inspectors need to carry a heavy Toughbook, a digital camera, and a printer with them to finish each inspection. They use Toughbook to check their daily schedule and enter vehicle data; they use digital camera to capture all required pictures; and they use printer to print out condition report.


To make our car inspectors’ life easier, we decided to replace Toughbook and digital camera by a smartphone. We are designing two apps (eventually, they will integrate into one app) which users can check schedule, update status, and capture data more accurately and efficiently in just one device.


So, these two apps are the two mobile design projects I am working on now:


AiM Schedule (In Production)

An android app which car inspectors can check their daily schedule and update their status


AiM Inspect (Pilot)

An Android app which car inspectors can collect all vehicle information (Trim info, tire, damage, condition, pictures, etc.) and upload data to cloud.


Additionally, I’m also working on the other web design project:


CRM

A website which our customer can request inspection online, dispatch team can schedule appointment, and data management team can manage all records.
